Bio

Michael Antony Officially known as D9Michael is a rising Tanzanian record producer in the Amapiano music scene. Known for his unique sound and energetic performances, D9Michael has quickly gained a loyal fanbase.He has performed mostly in East and that was when he started gaining more and more engagement. On 2nd February 2025, he released his mixtrack "Uyaphapha Bell" which is doing great. His music blends traditional Amapiano rhythms with modern influences, creating a fresh and exciting sound that resonates with audiences worldwide.
